About

Hindsight's musical journey began in the earlier part of 2022 when they embarked on their creative path with their debut song, THIS IS GOING TO SHIT. This marked the inception of a remarkable career that would soon transcend boundaries and genres. Not confined to just one facet of the music world, Hindsight also ventured into production at one point, showcasing their versatile talents. They crafted beats and sounds that became the foundation for a slew of renowned artists, including Frosttt, Tenebris, Spiiral, LxstAccustik, Chimera, Iftin Ewilthedemondude!, Rattlesnake, and Txaz, solidifying their reputation as not only a visionary artist but also a prolific producer.

Within the Necrotrap scene, Hindsight found their artistic home, and the collaborative spirit thrived as they frequently joined forces with their fellow scene, weaving a tapestry of dark and immersive sounds.
